Space Travel

By Joel Gonzales

The world is being evacuated because the solar system is collapsing on itself and world leaders are giving the people two choices of Space Travel.

The "First Class" form of space travel is for people willing to pay a premium; You get a much more classic looking spaceship.This spaceship also includes warp speed every two months.

You also recieve a personal butler and have the whole spaceship to yourself.

Price:$420,000,000,340,000,380.99

The "Economy Class" is for everyone else that can't afford first. You will be living in a giant spinning doughnut bunked with strangers.

The spaceship runs on coal.

Price:FREE
